I'm 19 years old, currently living in my sixth city (New York City) attending Columbia University.

Throughout my life, I've lived in six cities and two countries, constantly changing environments and adjusting to new communities. As a first-generation American, it is nice to call many different places home, and this experience has allowed me to appreciate the importance of fostering welcoming environments. Whether in school, at a work place, or at home, environments that encourage communication and collaboration will bring the best out people. This understanding has grown with me and has come to embody how I think encountering new people, ideas, situations, and challenges — a perspective I bring with me everywhere.
